How to make Chicken Pulao

Description

Are you in for a tasty lip-smacking and spicy treat? Chicken Pulao is the best dish for all your moods, occasions and parties, plus it is much easier than making Biryani!

Making Chicken Pulao is a quick and easy process. And I promise, it doesn't even make much of a mess in your kitchen!

All you need is a few ingredients, a pressure cooker and some music! Let's begin!

Ingredients:

  • 1 tbsp oil
  • 1 cinnamon stick
  • 4 cloves
  • 1-2 Bay leaves
  • 4 chopped onions
  • 3 tbsp ginger+garlic paste
  • 4 tsp red chilli powder
  • 1 tsp turmeric
  • 2 tsp Pulao Masala or Garam Masala
  • 1 kg chicken pieces
  • Some water
  • 2 cups of rice
  • Salt according to preference
  • Chopped cilantro leaves

Steps:

  1. Add 1 tbsp oil into the cooker
  2. Add 1 cinnamon stick, 4 cloves and 1-2 Bay leaves into the oil
  3. Add the chopped onions and wait for them to turn brown

    4. Wait...

  4. Then add 1 tsp turmeric
  5. Add 4 tsp red chilli powder
  6. Add 2 tsp Pulao Masala or Garam Masala
  7. Add 3 tbsp ginger+garlic paste
  8. Mix all the ingredients together
  9. Then add the chicken pieces
  10. Mix everything well
  11. Add 2 cups of rice
  12. Add 4 cups of water
  13. Add salt according to your preference
  14. Stir everything well
  15. Close the pressure cooker and wait for 15 minutes. Let the whistle go off 3 times and turn the stove off.

    16. Wait again...

  16. After the pressure has been released, open the cooker and check if everything is cooked
  17. Serve this in a plate and season it with chopped cilantro leaves!
Enjoy!
